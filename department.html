Computer Engineering Department Information

About Computer Engineering Department

Computer Engineering is a discipline that integrates several fields of electrical engineering and computer science required to develop computer hardware and software. It involves designing and testing computer components, such as circuits, processors, memory devices, and networks.

Courses Offered

  • Introduction to Computer Engineering
  • Computer Networks
  • Embedded Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • Computer Architecture

Facilities

The Computer Engineering Department offers state-of-the-art facilities including computer labs equipped with the latest hardware and software, research facilities, and collaboration spaces.

Faculty

The department is staffed with experienced faculty members who are experts in their respective fields. They are dedicated to providing quality education and conducting cutting-edge research.
